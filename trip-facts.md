## Group rules (verified FAQ - this is the "how many can come" constraint set)

1. **No group/team discount tier exists.** Every discounted seat is an individual application.
2. **2 tickets per order max.**
3. **GA ($699) can be bought for someone else** and is transferable.
4. **Discounted tickets (student/builder/resident) must be applied for AND claimed by the person attending** - non-transferable. You cannot stockpile cheap tickets for artists; each artist applies themselves.
5. Community discounts exist for groups like Protocol Guild members and open-source contributors.

**Implication:** group size is not capped by Devcon - it is capped by (a) each artist getting their own discount approved, and (b) money. Zaal cannot buy discounted seats for the crew; he CAN buy GA seats 2-per-order at $699 each.
