# ZAO Festivals - Tech Rider (v1 template)

Applies to Battle Set / Showcase / After-Party packages. Adapt per venue with the advance call (T-14) and mandatory soundcheck (T-5 or day-of, 90 min before doors).

## Audio

- PA appropriate to room (we can source rental if needed; typical market $225-1,600/day)
- 2x wireless handheld mics (MC + artist), 2x backup wired SM58
- DJ booth: 2-channel minimum mixer position, 2x XLR or RCA inputs
- Artist playback: 3.5mm/USB-C aux + Bluetooth fallback at FOH
- 2x stage monitors (battles are vocal-forward; artists need to hear themselves)
- House music feed accessible for walk-in/transitions

## Power + Internet

- 2x 20A circuits at stage, 1x at streaming table
- HARD REQUIREMENT for onchain mechanics + simulcast: dedicated wired ethernet (10 Mbps up minimum, 25+ preferred) at streaming table, or verified dedicated WiFi SSID. Venue WiFi shared with 500 attendees does not count.
- Backup: we carry a 5G hotspot; venue cell coverage confirmed in advance

## Streaming table (we bring)

- 2x laptops (encoder + mechanics/voting), capture card, camera (or feed from house cams if available), audio feed from FOH (XLR or USB)
- Need: 6ft table, 2 chairs, sightline to stage, the wired drop above

## Stage / Room

- Battle format: two artist positions + MC center; minimum 12ft x 8ft performable area
- Projector or LED screen with HDMI input for live vote display + QR codes (we supply content laptop)
- Basic stage wash lighting; we adapt to house rig, no movers required
- Changeovers are 5-12 min; we run our own stage manager

## Onchain mechanics

- Audience participates by phone (QR to vote/collect) - no wallet required for entry-level voting, wallet optional for collectibles
- We handle all contracts/infra; nothing deploys on venue systems
- Signage: 2-4 QR placards, we print

## Crew + Access

- Crew of 3-6 depending on package; load-in 3-4 hours before doors (after-party) or 90 min (battle set)
- 1x parking/load spot, green room or holding space for artists, water
- Local runner appreciated for 200+ person events, not required
