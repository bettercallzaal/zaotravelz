# 2027: The Year of the ZAO Festivals

The campaign: 2026 closes the proof leg (ZAOstock, Devcon 8, Art Basel). 2027 runs the full circuit - ZAO Festivals plugs into every major web3, music, tech, and streaming room, quarter by quarter, with the live onchain battle as the signature and the artist houses traveling with it.

**Operating rule (locked 2026-07-03): ZAO ORGANIZES exactly one event - ZAOstock, the flagship. Every other stop on the circuit is a PARTNERSHIP: we bring the music/battles/artists/streaming to someone else's event. We are the entertainment layer, never the competing organizer.** This keeps the crew light, the calendar survivable, and makes every conference an ally instead of a rival.
