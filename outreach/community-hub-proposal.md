## Hub name

**The Independent Music Hub** (alt: Onchain Music Hub / Real World Music Hub)

## Topic (one Ethereum topic, as required)

Independent artist economics on Ethereum: how musicians reclaim profit margin, data, and IP rights using open-source, onchain rails - splits, tokenized communities, transparent payouts, and verifiable rights.

## Why this hub, why now

- Devcon 8's own framing is "Real World Ethereum." Music is the most human real-world use case: every attendee has artists they love, and most artists earn almost nothing from streaming.
- No previous Devcon has had a music-focused Community Hub. India is one of the world's largest music markets (Bollywood + a massive independent scene) - the audience walking past this hub lives this problem.
- The tooling matured: 0xSplits, onchain ticketing, token communities, instant artist payouts - all real, all demoable, all open source.
- Post-Sound.xyz-shutdown (Jan 2026), the "what now for onchain music" conversation needs a home. This hub is it.

## What happens in the hub (4-day programming skeleton)

| Slot | Format | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Daily 10am | Workshop: artist onboarding | First wallet to first split: musician goes from zero to receiving an onchain payout, live |
| Daily 12pm | Case study session | 90+ weeks of music-community governance: what worked, what failed (Respect Game data, public) |
| Daily 2pm | Open build | Bring your artist / bring your repo: splits, ticketing, token-gating clinics with mentors |
| Daily 4pm | Live demo | Live onchain music battle: two artists, audience votes, transparent instant payout - the mechanics explained as they run |
| Day 4 close | Community session | India x global independent music roundtable with local artists + builders |

All educational, no token shilling, no project marketing - format teaches MECHANISMS (splits, payouts, governance) with our tools as one example among many (we present alongside 0xSplits docs, open standards, and other community projects).
