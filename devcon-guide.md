Companion to `trip-facts.md`. Full research: ZAOOS docs 945 + 946.

## What Devcon is

Ethereum Foundation's flagship conference since 2014. Lineage: Berlin (0, 2014) - London (1) - Shanghai (2, the DoS war-room one) - Cancun (3) - Prague (4, ETH2 roadmap unveiled) - Osaka (5) - Bogota (6, post-covid) - Bangkok (7 SEA, 2024: 12,500 people, 130+ countries, 60% FIRST-TIMERS) - Mumbai (8, first ever India/South Asia).

Why Mumbai: India is the largest source of new crypto devs since 2023 (17% of all new global crypto devs in 2024). EF timed it days before Diwali (Sun Nov 8) on purpose - stay through it.

Culture rules veterans repeat:
- Hallway track beats talks. Every talk since 2014 is free forever at archive.devcon.org - watch on the flight home.
- Side events are where connections happen. Telegram groups (spin up Aug-Sept) coordinate everything.
- 5 meaningful conversations per day beats 20 sessions.
- Smallest VC-to-builder ratio of any major crypto event. Dress casual.
- 60% of the room is new too. "I'm new here, any advice?" works.

## Mumbai ground truth

- **Stay Bandra West**, not BKC. BKC is offices, dead at night. Bandra West = cafes, venues, 5-10 min Uber (₹150-250 / $2-3) to Jio World Centre. Book by early Sept - Diwali surges lodging 40-60%.
- **Airport to BKC:** Metro Line 3 (fully open), 19 min, ₹20 (~25 cents), every 4-8 min. Uber $4-6.
- **Getting around:** Metro Line 3 spine (airport-BKC-South Mumbai) + Uber/Ola. Autos banned in South Mumbai. Avoid trains 8-10am / 5-8pm.
- **Music venues to hit:** antiSOCIAL (Lower Parel - THE indie/electronic hub, 450 cap), Bonobo + Veranda (Bandra West, live jazz/indie), NCPA (Nariman Point, institutional). No festival that week; check Bandsintown in Oct.
- **Diwali Nov 8:** fireworks, lights, festive city right after Devcon ends. Traffic heavy Nov 6-8, closures Nov 8-9. Fly home Nov 9.
- **Weather:** dry, 82-93F. Cotton + sunscreen + light jacket.
- **Money:** UPI One World tourist wallet now works for US visitors (passport + visa at airport kiosk, load up to ₹50K / $600) - scan any UPI QR like a local. Carry ₹5-10K cash for street food.
- **SIM:** Jio kiosk at BOM arrivals: ₹299 (~$3.50) for 28 days, 1.5GB/day + unlimited 5G, 15 min setup.
- **Safety:** bottled water only, fresh-cooked street food fine (vada pav ₹15-30, eat it), app-cabs only, Bandra/BKC/Colaba safe. Very vegetarian-friendly city.
